Privacy Policy — Plus 10

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Plus 10 ("the app") is a card-game scorekeeper. We designed it to be private by default. This policy explains what the app does — and doesn't do — with your information.

Information we collect

None. Plus 10 does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. We have no servers, no analytics, and no accounts.

Data you enter

Player names, games, groups, and scores that you enter are stored only on your device. This data never leaves your device, is not sent to us or any third party, and is not accessible to us. If you delete the app, this data is removed.

Internet and third parties

The app does not require an internet connection to function and contains no advertising, no third-party tracking, and no third-party software development kits that collect data.

Children's privacy

Plus 10 does not collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
